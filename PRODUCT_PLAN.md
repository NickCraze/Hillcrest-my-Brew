# Hillcrest MyBrew — Product & Business Model Plan

## 1. Vision

Hillcrest MyBrew aims to become the **digital operating system of local communities**, enabling residents, small businesses, and initiatives to discover, connect, transact, and participate within their geographic area.

The platform is designed around the principle that **community strength grows from visibility, awareness, and participation at the local level**.

---

## 2. Mission

* Increase visibility for small local businesses and initiatives
* Reduce informational fragmentation across WhatsApp, Facebook, flyers, and word-of-mouth
* Enable discovery of events, deals, and opportunities within a geographic area
* Strengthen local participation and community engagement
* Provide practical utility (e.g., ordering, discovery, coordination) within a suburb or city

---

## 3. Problem Statement

### 3.1 Residents

* Do not know what is happening locally
* Miss events, specials, and opportunities
* Feel disconnected from their suburb or city
* Information is fragmented across multiple channels

### 3.2 Small Businesses

* Limited marketing reach
* Dependence on social platforms with low locality targeting
* Difficulty driving foot traffic
* Lack of affordable local discovery channels

### 3.3 Community Initiatives

* Low visibility
* Difficulty recruiting volunteers
* Poor coordination mechanisms

---

## 4. Solution Overview

Hillcrest MyBrew is a **location-aware community platform** that enables users to:

* Discover local events, deals, and community updates
* Explore businesses and activities within their area
* Participate in community initiatives
* Access local discovery via feed and map
* Place pre-orders for pickup at local vendors

The platform combines:

* Community feed
* Event discovery
* Local deals marketplace
* Map-based exploration
* On-demand pickup ordering

---

## 5. Target Market

### 5.1 Primary Users

* Residents within metropolitan areas
* Young professionals
* Families
* Fitness and social community participants

### 5.2 Secondary Users

* Cafes and restaurants
* Gyms and studios
* Event organizers
* Clubs and social groups
* Community organizations

### 5.3 Initial Geography

* Launch metro: Durban / KZN region
* Initial density suburb: Hillcrest (content seeding)
* Expansion: Greater Durban → Other SA metros

---

## 6. Core Product Concept

### 6.1 Intent-Based Home

Users select primary intent upon entry:

* What’s happening (community feed)
* Coffee / food pickup
* Deals & specials
* Explore area

This reduces navigation friction and supports multiple user needs.

---

## 7. Location Architecture

### Hierarchy

* Province
* Metro / City
* Suburb
* Venue

### Location Behavior

* User selects metro and suburb on onboarding
* Content scoped to suburb with ability to expand to metro
* Optional GPS detection with user confirmation

* A community coordination system
